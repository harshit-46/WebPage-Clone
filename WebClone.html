Sound Quality &
Performance

On ear closed back headphones are compromised designs
(as opposed to over ear models), but the sound quality of
the Beats Solo 3 is really fun. Of course they are bass
heavy (very heavy), and midrange is also boosted
noticeably with a lack in the upper frequencies. This
equates to a very punchy, powerful sound – which while
not accurate, is definitely fun to listen to.
